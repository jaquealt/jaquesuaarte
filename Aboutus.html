About us

My name is Jaque, and since 1994 I have been passionate about technology. It was in that year that I graduated from high school and took my first steps in the field. Since then, I have closely followed the incredible technological evolution we have witnessed. I have a degree in Information Systems, which has provided me with a solid foundation for my career in the field.

My professional trajectory in technology encompasses various areas. I started working in electronic publishing and then delved into programming. I also had the opportunity to teach classes in basic computer skills, the Office suite, and Corel Draw. Later on, I entered the field of IT, where I dedicated myself to maintaining networks, servers, and computers functioning flawlessly in the companies I worked for. Furthermore, I worked in customer support and assistance for ERP systems. Throughout this journey, I also acquired skills in video and image editing, as well as experience in website creation.

Today, I am venturing into the field of artificial intelligence, seeking to expand my knowledge in this fascinating area. Learning new technologies has always been a pleasure for me, and I see each challenge as an opportunity for growth. I am always open to exploring new systems and discovering innovative ways to apply technology in everyday life.
